---

## The 12 Move Grammar

Every prompt in this library is built on one of 12 structural moves
derived from analysis of all three original Oblique Strategies editions.

| # | Move | Core Action |
|---|------|-------------|
| 1 | Subtraction | Remove, reduce, strip to essence |
| 2 | Inversion | Flip polarity, reverse direction |
| 3 | Constraint | Impose an arbitrary rule that closes the obvious path |
| 4 | Displacement | Shift medium, domain, speed, or scale |
| 5 | Attention | Direct focus to what's being ignored |
| 6 | Acceptance | Reframe a problem as a resource |
| 7 | Perspective | Inhabit a genuinely alien point of view |
| 8 | Time | Disrupt temporal relationship to the work |
| 9 | Reduction | Find the smallest unit and work only from there |
| 10 | Courage | Remove the permission-seeking. Just go. |
| 11 | Process | Trust motion over destination |
| 12 | Reality Check | Cut abstraction. Look at what's actually there. |
