Privacy

Reflect does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

It makes no network connections. It has no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising identifiers, no third-party SDKs. Everything happens on your device.

On devices with Apple Intelligence, Reflect uses Apple's Foundation Models to generate new prompts. That generation runs entirely on-device. No prompt, generated or curated, is ever sent to a server.

There is no data to access, export, or delete, because there is no data.
